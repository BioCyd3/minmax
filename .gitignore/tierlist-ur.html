Complete UR Palmon Tier List

A detailed whitepaper-level analysis of all Ultra Rare Pals, including skills, star upgrades, team compositions, strategic value, and investment priority.

S+ Tier: Meta-Defining Game Changers

Barkplug

Electricity UR Front Row

Summary

The ultimate offensive force multiplier. Barkplug fundamentally changes every fight by making your entire team more powerful.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Ion Disruption): The source of its power. All enemies suffer a permanent -20% Defense debuff.
  • Rage (Overcharge): Deals 2331% damage and can Paralyze a key target.

Strategic Analysis

  • Synergies: Pairs with everyone, especially high-damage AoE pals like Mantleray or single-target nukers like Lucidina.
  • Counters: Vulnerable to focus fire and back-row assassins like Hoofrit.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars increase the Defense Reduction of its basic attack (up to +10%).

Verdict: Extremely High Value. Every star directly enhances its core role as a debuffer, making even its basic attacks contribute to the team's overall damage amplification. A prime investment target.

Statchew

Earth UR Front Row

Summary

The ultimate defensive anchor. Statchew's purpose is to make your front line unbreakable, enabling any strategy.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Sentinel): Its cornerstone ability. All allied front-row Pals take a constant -20% damage.
  • Rage (Protective Roar): Cripples enemy front-row damage with a -13.5% damage debuff.

Strategic Analysis

  • Synergies: Protects fragile back-liners and makes other front-line bruisers like Dolphriend incredibly durable.
  • Counters: Bypassed by back-row assassins (Hoofrit). Defense shred from Barkplug can soften it.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars provide a colossal increase to its basic attack damage (up to +270%).

Verdict: Very High Value. While its base kit is defensive, stars transform Statchew into a terrifying damage-dealing tank. This makes him a dual-threat that enemies cannot ignore. An excellent, game-changing investment.

S Tier: Elite Role-Players & Strategic Pillars

Lucidina

Water UR Back Row

Summary

The queen of single-target elimination, designed to remove the most dangerous enemy with extreme prejudice.

Skill Breakdown

  • Rage (Torment): Deals a staggering 2590% damage and makes the target receive +10% more damage.
  • Passive (Dream Shadow): A devastating +30% Crit Rate.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional Crit Rate (up to +10%).

Verdict: High Value. Each star makes her core function as a hyper-carry even more consistent and deadly. A fantastic investment for players who need a reliable boss-killer.

Hoofrit

Fire UR Front Row

Summary

The premier back-row assassin, bypassing tanks to neutralize the enemy's vulnerable core.

Skill Breakdown

  • Rage (Blazing Volley): Game-winning skill. Deals 2497.5% damage to the enemy's back row with a 60% stun chance.
  • Passive (Hard-Horned): Gains +30% max HP.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional HP (up to +10%).

Verdict: High Value. More HP directly enhances its ability to survive on the front line, making it more likely to successfully execute its game-winning Rage skill. A very safe and effective investment.

Surveilynx

Earth UR Front Row

Summary

A unique strategic pillar whose true power lies in bolstering your entire camp's defense system.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Elite Drills): Its S-Tier purpose. Allows Quick Training of 1000 Armigo/day and gives all defending Armigo +20% Attack.
  • Rage (Shadow Step): A solid 1387.5% damage fan-shaped AoE for clearing waves.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant bonus Attack to your Armigo (up to +10%).

Verdict: Extremely High Value. Like Incineraptor, investing in Surveilynx is an investment in a core game system. Each star directly strengthens your base defense, a permanent and powerful upgrade.

Fingenue

Water UR Back Row

Summary

A pure and devastatingly effective back-line AoE damage dealer whose power scales incredibly well.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Ocean's Favor): A rare and powerful +30% final damage dealt multiplier.
  • Rage (Waterspout): A large 1803.75% AoE damage nuke.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional Final Damage (up to +10%).

Verdict: Extremely High Value. Final Damage is the best damage stat in most games. Stacking it via stars makes Fingenue one of the highest-scaling damage dealers available. A top-tier investment.

A Tier: Elite Combat Powerhouses

Magmolin

Fire UR Front Row

Summary

A front-line tank with one of the most powerful single-target nukes in the game and a useful economic bonus.

Skill Breakdown

  • Rage (Lava Shock): An incredible 2775% damage to a single enemy.
  • Passive (Tempered Steel): Gains +30% HP and doubles the Steel output of all Camp Furnaces.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional HP (up to +10%).

Verdict: High Value. As a front-liner, more HP is critical for survival. Living longer means it's more likely to unleash its devastating Rage skill. The Furnace bonus is a great perk.

Ninjump

Water UR Front Row

Summary

A straightforward and highly effective front-line damage dealer that excels at clearing waves.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Tidal Strike): A simple and effective +30% Attack boost.
  • Rage (Strike Like a Waterfall): Flings 3 shuriken in a fan, each dealing 1248.75% damage.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional Attack (up to +10%).

Verdict: High Value. It's a pure damage dealer. More attack from stars directly enhances its one and only job. A reliable and strong investment.

Blazeal

Fire UR Front Row

Summary

A pure damage machine, designed to blast through enemy lines with a powerful wave of fire.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Tempered Steel): A clean +30% Attack buff.
  • Rage (Huuuah!): Releases a wave dealing 1665% damage to all enemies in its path.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional Attack (up to +10%).

Verdict: High Value. Just like Ninjump, investing in Blazeal's stars directly boosts its raw power. If you need a fire-type damage powerhouse, he's a great investment.

Dolphriend

Water UR Front Row

Summary

A premier front-line bruiser specializing in neutralizing the enemy's biggest threat with damage and control.

Skill Breakdown

  • Rage (Rush Wave): Huge 2109% damage nuke with a 70% chance to stun for 3s.
  • Passive (Tidal Guard): Gains +30% max HP.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional HP (up to +10%).

Verdict: High Value. More HP increases its durability and ensures it can perform its role as a disruptive bruiser effectively. A solid investment for a reliable front-liner.

Baboom

Earth UR Front Row

Summary

A very effective tanky bruiser with a powerful stun and the potential to lead mono-Earth teams.

Skill Breakdown

  • Rage (Powerstrike): A high-damage 2220% strike with a 70% chance to stun for 2s.
  • Passive (Nature's Gift): Grants all Earth Palmon Attack. (Note: Data shows 0%, likely a typo for a scaling value).

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars grant additional Defense (up to +10%).

Verdict: Good Value. More defense makes it a better front-line anchor, increasing its survivability so it can reliably stun key targets. Its value skyrockets if the passive is fixed/buffed.

Abuzzinian

Electricity UR Back Row

Summary

A slippery back-line disruptor that relies on Evasion and multi-target paralysis to cause chaos.

Skill Breakdown

  • Passive (Phantomstep): Gains a massive +30% Evasion, making it hard to hit.
  • Rage (Nightstorm): Hits 3 random enemies for 1387.5% damage with a chance to Paralyze for 3.5s.

Star Upgrade Path & Investment Value

Upgrades: Stars provide a large increase to its basic attack damage (up to +270%).

Verdict: Medium Value. While more damage is always good, it doesn't enhance its primary strengths of survivability (Evasion) or control (Paralysis chance). A decent investment, but lower priority than URs who get core stats from stars.
